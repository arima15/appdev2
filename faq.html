Frequently Asked Questions

Clinic Hours

The school clinic is open from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 1:00 PM to 4:00 PM.

FAQs

Does the cost get added to my school fees?

No, the cost of clinic services is not added to your school fees. You will be billed separately for any services rendered.

Can I volunteer to help at the clinic?

Yes, students can volunteer to help at the clinic. Please contact the clinic administration for more details on how to apply.

Does the clinic do other dental care aside from dental checkups?

Yes, the clinic offers a range of dental services including cleanings, fillings, and extractions. Please schedule an appointment for specific services.

What should I do in case of a medical emergency?

In case of a medical emergency, please contact the campus security or go to the nearest hospital immediately.

How can I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment through the Clinic Appointment System on our website or by visiting the clinic in person.

Are walk-ins allowed?

Walk-ins are allowed but it is highly recommended to schedule an appointment to ensure timely service.
